Privacy Policy

BarberTime - Premium Barbershop

Effective Date: May 21, 2026

Last Updated: May 21, 2026

1. Introduction

BarberTime ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our appointment booking services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when booking an appointment, including:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Service Information: Type of haircut or service requested
  • Communication Preferences: SMS and/or Email consent
  • Technical Information: Browser type, IP address, pages visited, visit duration

3. SMS Communication & TCPA Compliance

Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA) - 47 U.S.C. § 227

By checking the SMS consent box, you:
  • Provide express written consent to receive SMS messages from BarberTime
  • Agree to receive appointment confirmations, reminders, and updates via text message
  • Acknowledge that message frequency may vary (estimated 1-2 messages per appointment)
  • Understand that standard message and data rates apply
  • Agree that SMS consent is not a condition of service

3.1 SMS Opt-Out Procedure

You can unsubscribe from SMS messages at any time by replying "STOP" to any text message we send. You will receive a confirmation that you have been unsubscribed. This will not affect your appointment unless you request cancellation.

3.2 SMS Message Practices

  • All SMS messages include "Reply STOP to opt out"
  • Messages are sent only to phone numbers you provide
  • We do not share your phone number with third parties for marketing
  • Messages are sent between 9 AM - 9 PM your local time
  • All SMS logs are retained for 2 years for compliance purposes

3.3 TCPA Compliance Standards

  • ✅ Express written consent obtained before sending SMS
  • ✅ Opt-out mechanism available on every message
  • ✅ Calls/texts only during reasonable hours (9 AM - 9 PM local time)
  • ✅ No use of automated calls or texts without prior written consent
  • ✅ Do Not Call registry honored for all outbound calls
  • ✅ Caller ID displays "BarberTime" or our business name

4. Email Communication & CAN-SPAM Compliance

CAN-SPAM Act - 15 U.S.C. § 7701

By checking the Email consent box, you:
  • Provide consent to receive appointment confirmations and reminders via email
  • Acknowledge receipt of this Privacy Policy
  • Allow us to send promotional emails (only if opted in)
  • Understand you can unsubscribe at any time

4.1 Email Practices

  • All marketing emails include an unsubscribe link
  • Transactional emails (confirmations, reminders) do not require opt-in
  • We respond to unsubscribe requests within 10 business days
  • Our business address is included in every email
  • Email headers are truthful and not misleading

4.2 CAN-SPAM Compliance Standards

  • ✅ Clear and accurate subject line (no misleading content)
  • ✅ Identification as advertisement or promotion (if applicable)
  • ✅ Clear business name and physical address in email footer
  • ✅ Functional unsubscribe link in every email
  • ✅ Monitor third-party email marketing services for compliance
  • ✅ Respond to unsubscribe requests within 10 business days
  • ✅ Email footer contains: BarberTime | 123 Main Street | Your City, State 12345

4.3 Email Unsubscribe

Click the "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any email from us. We will remove you from our mailing list within 10 business days.

5. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To confirm and process your appointment booking
  • To send appointment reminders via SMS and/or Email (based on your consent)
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • To improve our website and services
  • To comply with legal obligations and maintain audit trails
  • To prevent fraud and illegal activity

6. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We may share your information with:

  • Twilio: SMS delivery service (uses your phone number and message content)
  • SendGrid: Email delivery service (uses your email and message content)
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or court order

We DO NOT sell your personal data to third parties.

7. Data Storage & Security

Your information is stored securely using industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL). We implement reasonable security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure database encryption at rest
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access controls and authentication requirements
  • Employee training on data protection and privacy

Data Retention: We retain your information for 2 years after your last appointment, or as required by law, then securely delete it.

8. GDPR Compliance (EU & EEA Residents)

General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

8.1 Your GDPR Rights

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("Right to be Forgotten")
  • Right to Data Portability: Download your data in a structured format
  • Right to Object: Opt-out of data processing
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data

8.2 Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:

  • Consent: You explicitly consent via our appointment form
  • Contract Necessity: Data required to provide services
  • Legal Obligation: Data retention for compliance and audit

8.3 Data Subject Rights Requests

To exercise your GDPR rights, email us at: privacy@barbertime.com

Response timeframe: 30 calendar days (extendable to 90 days for complex requests)

9. CCPA Compliance (California Residents)

California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under CCPA § 1798.100:

9.1 Your CCPA Rights

  • Right to Know: What personal information is collected, used, and shared
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information collected
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of the sale or sharing of personal information
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Receive non-discriminatory treatment for exercising rights

9.2 CCPA Compliance Standards

  • ✅ No Data Sales: We do not sell personal information as defined by CCPA
  • ✅ Service Provider Agreements: Twilio and SendGrid are data processors, not sellers
  • ✅ Consumer Rights: All above rights are honored and documented
  • ✅ Non-Discrimination: No differential pricing or service quality
  • ✅ Response Timeframe: 45 calendar days for rights requests
  • ✅ Parental Consent: No data collected from those under 16 without parental consent

9.3 California Resident Rights Requests

Submit requests to: privacy@barbertime.com

Response timeframe: 45 calendar days
